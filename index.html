About Skymingle

Skymingle is a privacy-minded, browser-native chat and video demo for small groups and prototyping. It uses localStorage for persistence, BroadcastChannel for cross-tab real-time sync, and WebRTC for peer-to-peer video calls.

Multi-room Chat

Create and switch between rooms. Messages are stored per room locally.

In-Browser Video

Peer-to-peer video powered by WebRTC — no central server required for local testing.

Privacy-first

All data remains in the browser (localStorage). No external servers are contacted unless you enable them.

Search-ready

Structured data and meta tags included for better indexing and preview on social platforms.

How to Use

  1. Set a nickname and create or select a room.
  2. Send messages — they persist per room and sync across your browser tabs.
  3. Start a video call to share camera and audio with peers in the same room (tabs only for now).

Privacy & Verification

Skymingle runs entirely in your browser. When you register your site with Google/Bing, paste the provided verification tokens into the head meta placeholders. For production, host the files under a stable HTTPS URL and submit a sitemap.
